Laboratory Work №4: Behavioral Diagrams
This section contains Behavioral Diagrams (Sequence and State Diagrams) illustrating the dynamic interactions and life cycles within the systems.

Dynamic Models
1. Car Insurance System
Focus: Accident claim processing and approval workflow.
Logic: Models the sequence of communication between the Policyholder, Adjuster, and Insurance Database.

2. ATM System
Focus: Transaction verification and cash withdrawal logic.
Logic: Defines the step-by-step interaction between the User, ATM Hardware, and Bank Server.

3. Medical Clinic System
Focus: Appointment booking and patient record updates.
Logic: Illustrates the life cycle of a medical appointment, from scheduling to completion.
